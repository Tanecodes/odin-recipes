Ramen

yummy ramen

these are the ingredients to make this delicious ramen meal

ingredients

  • yumyum noodles
  • 1 egg
  • vegetables
  • pork,lamb or beef(personal preference)

steps to make the meal

  1. firstly boil your vegetables via pot
  2. start cutting your meat into bits and place in pot to boil
  3. shortly after boiling for a while add noodles into pot
  4. add seasoning from noodles
  5. boil your egg in seperate pot
  6. remove shell from egg and place in pot
  7. let food simmer for 5mins
  8. poor into bowl and eat with chopsticks
