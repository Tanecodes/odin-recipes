Butter chicken

how to make butter chicken

these are the ingredients to create butterchicken

ingredients

  • chicken breast
  • butterchicken sauce
  • rice

steps to make meal

  1. start by cutting chicken breast into bits
  2. put in pan to cook with olive oil
  3. once browned add butterchicken sauce and let it simmer
  4. while simmering cook rice in rice cooker or pot of water
  5. once chicken is cooked add to a plate with cooked rice underneath the butterchicken and done
  6. enjoy meal
