cheeseburger

how to make a cheeseburger

these are the ingredients

  • meat patty
  • cheese slice
  • sliced piece of tomato
  • burger buns

steps to make meal

  1. cook the meat patty in a pan for 10mins
  2. add seasoning to meat
  3. toast buns if you wish
  4. add patty to the buns
  5. slice a tomato piece add cheese to burger first then tomato
  6. add sauces if you wish
  7. enjoy
